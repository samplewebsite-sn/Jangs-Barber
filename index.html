About Jang's Barber Shop

Since 1994

Jang's Barber Shop in Hillcrest, Auckland is a friendly local place for quality haircuts, fades, and beard trims. Jang takes pride in his work and enjoys making every customer look and feel their best. Outside the shop, he loves spending time at church, listening to music, fishing, painting, playing golf, and working on his farm.
